What & who we are

Failnet is an collective gathered and dedicated to these morals in which we shall always defend and never stray...

  • Full and free transparency in information unless it is detrimental to the anonymity of others or ourselves
  • We will live by and enforce the virtues of the hacker ethic.
  • We will never EVER sell and/or turn over any user data to any agency/company/government body.
  • We will NEVER endorse or support ANY form racism, sexism, or other discrimination.
  • We will NEVER endorse, support, or defend child pornography, pedophilia, or ANY form of child abuse, we do believe there is a cure and that cure is called .45 ACP. we will always actively pursue pedophiles and hand them over to the law.
  • WE will never stand or tolerate tyranny, or any unjust government we believe people are free they are not numbers people should not be slaves.
  • We will always support freedom, free as in free beer.
  • Everyone is entiled to Privacy
  • We will always support Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) we will never use any paid or proprietary software.

Anyone who supports these ideas is a member of the community and is welcome. We are not connected in any way shape or form to any other group/company/organization or government. We are run solely by volenteers.
